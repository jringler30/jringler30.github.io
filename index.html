01

About

I work at the intersection of analytics and decision-making — building tools that help teams move from ambiguity to action.

My focus is on the systems behind good decisions: optimization engines, analytical dashboards, and ML prototypes designed to surface what matters and filter out what doesn't. At Wayfair, that meant commercial strategy analytics informing category-level pricing. At Bell Flight, operational modeling for aviation programs. In my current capstone with Vise, it means building portfolio optimization and tax-loss harvesting systems for real-world deployment.

I'm drawn to problems that sit between technical complexity and business judgment — where the answer isn't just a model output, but a recommendation someone can act on.
