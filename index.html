About

I design and build decision tools — analytics dashboards, optimization models, and machine-learning prototypes — that help teams move from ambiguity to action. My work sits at the intersection of analytics engineering and strategic problem-solving: I translate messy, cross-functional questions into structured analyses and clear recommendations.

Whether it's constructing a tax-loss harvesting optimizer, parsing network data for community structure, or forecasting portfolio performance, I focus on building tools that are useful on day one — clean, reproducible, and trusted by the stakeholders who rely on them.

I'm drawn to roles where structured thinking meets real-world impact: consulting, analytics, product strategy, and anywhere that rigorous analysis shapes better outcomes.
